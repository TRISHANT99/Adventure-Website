Everest Camp Trek

It was really great trekking to EBC and will always be one of my favourite ones. The whole trek was beautiful walking through the green forests, and then going above the tree lines, through the rocky calm valleys. And what made the trek even more memorable is the monsoon time, as the clouds danced along the huge mountains making the views become more than a dream. And the guide allotted was really knowledgeable as he explained well throughout the trek about the summits, the local stories, rituals and many more. Glad I finally made this trek as it now sprouts many more dreams within. Thanks Thrillophilia for organizing my first international trip especially to EBC!!
